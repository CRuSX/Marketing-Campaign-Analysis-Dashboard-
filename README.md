# Marketing-Campaign-Analysis-Dashboard-
Developed a Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to evaluate revenue, spend, ROI,  conversions, and impressions. 
This Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of marketing performance across different campaigns, regions, and industries. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key metrics, evaluate campaign effectiveness, and identify areas for optimization.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Average ROI (67.63%) – Measures the overall return generated from marketing investments.
Total Revenue (42.54M) – Total revenue generated from all marketing campaigns.
Total Spend (25.69M) – Total budget spent across campaigns.
Total Conversions (1M) – Number of successful customer conversions.
Total Impressions (503M) – Total audience reach across all campaigns.
Visualizations
1. Total Spend and Total Revenue by Region
Compares marketing spend against revenue generated across different regions.
Helps identify the most profitable geographic markets.
Shows that regions with higher investments generally generate higher revenue.
2. Total Spend and Average ROI by Campaign Name
Displays campaign spending alongside ROI percentages.
Enables comparison of campaign efficiency and profitability.
Helps determine which campaigns deliver the best return on investment.
3. Sum of ROI by Campaign Name
Ranks campaigns based on their total ROI contribution.
Highlights top-performing campaigns and identifies underperforming ones.
4. Count of Total Campaigns by Campaign Type
Shows the distribution of campaigns across campaign categories (Digital vs Traditional).
Provides insights into marketing channel allocation.
5. Total ROI and Average ROI Gauge
Summarizes overall ROI performance.
Offers a quick assessment of campaign effectiveness against target benchmarks.
Interactive Filters
Industry Filter: Analyze campaign performance for specific industries such as Automotive, Education, Finance, Healthcare, Retail, and Tech.
Campaign Name Filter: Focus on individual campaign performance for detailed analysis.
Business Value
Tracks marketing effectiveness in real time.
Supports data-driven budget allocation decisions.
Identifies high-performing campaigns and regions.
Helps optimize marketing strategies to maximize ROI and revenue growth.

Tools Used: Power BI, Data Visualization, Marketing Analytics, Business Intelligence.
