📊 Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard

🚀 Developed a Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to evaluate Revenue, Spend, ROI, Conversions, and Impressions. This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of marketing performance across different campaigns, regions, and industries, helping stakeholders monitor key metrics, evaluate campaign effectiveness, and identify opportunities for optimization.

🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
💹 Average ROI (67.63%) – Measures the overall return generated from marketing investments.
💰 Total Revenue (42.54M) – Total revenue generated from all marketing campaigns.
💸 Total Spend (25.69M) – Total budget spent across campaigns.
🎯 Total Conversions (1M) – Number of successful customer conversions.
👀 Total Impressions (503M) – Total audience reach across all campaigns.
📈 Visualizations
🌍 1. Total Spend and Total Revenue by Region
Compares marketing spend against revenue generated across different regions.
Identifies the most profitable geographic markets.
Highlights the relationship between investment and revenue generation.
📊 2. Total Spend and Average ROI by Campaign Name
Displays campaign spending alongside ROI percentages.
Enables comparison of campaign efficiency and profitability.
Helps determine which campaigns deliver the highest returns.
🏆 3. Sum of ROI by Campaign Name
Ranks campaigns based on their total ROI contribution.
Highlights top-performing campaigns.
Identifies campaigns that may require optimization.
📢 4. Count of Total Campaigns by Campaign Type
Shows the distribution of campaigns across Digital and Traditional marketing channels.
Provides insights into marketing channel allocation and strategy.
🎯 5. Total ROI and Average ROI Gauge
Summarizes overall ROI performance.
Offers a quick assessment of campaign effectiveness against performance targets.
